Valentine Party for the Howell Group Home

This event is alot fun for the community. The girls get together and organise an event for the Howell Group Home. There are mini games and prizes to be won as well as food and drink provided by the troop.The girls assist the needy and it is an overall great experience for everyone.

Mini Games

  • Ring Toss
  • Cornhole
  • Three Legged Race

Date: This even takes place on February 14th

Cookie Booths

Cookie Booths are a really fun and great time for the scouts and the community. The girls set up booths outside and pitch the girlscout cookies to people in the hopes that they will buy some. This exersize builds communication and buisness skills for the girls and helps the troop get money to put on events.

Dates:

  • May 15th
  • May 30th
  • June 15th
  • June 30th
  • July 15th

Camp Graham

Camp Graham is a amazing girlscout camp that helps the girls learn basic camping skills and also earn merit badges they need to advance ion the troop.

Camp Activities

  • Learn boating skills and swimming skill at the waterfront.
  • Learn first-aid and life saving skills in the medical section.
  • Go on amazing hikes through the forests and engage with the local wildlife.
  • Learn Mountain biking and earn the Mountain Biking merit badge.

Dates: June 20th-27th

Mt Crowder Hike

This event is where the girls get to go to Mt Crowder and hike to the top. There are two groups, The advanced group which takes the more difficult path up the mountain and then there is the the beginner group which takes the easier path up the mountain.

This trip allows the girls to learn how to hike and also allows them to gain an amazing experience because there is nothing quite as satisfying as reaching the top of the mountain and the view isnt half bad either.

Date: August 15th
