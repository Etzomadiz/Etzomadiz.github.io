Welcome

Welcome to troop 555's website. The troop leader is Paulet Shaw. She has been the groups leader for 10 + years and hopes to do it for 10 more. Troop 555 is always looking for perspective girls to join the troop. To join troop 555 the perspective Girl Scout must be in highschool and within the Mecklenberg county.

Joining troop 555 will assure you to be indocturinated into the GirlScout family. 555 teaches life skills and how to give back to the community.
