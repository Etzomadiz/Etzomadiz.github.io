Everyday Maintainence

There are a lot of things that everyone can do around their house every day that sometimes can get overlooked. You can start by cleaning out the filters and drains in your house. If you have an AC like 90% of homes have nowadays then you may want to consider changing out your air filters that are located in the massive grates in the ceiling in your house. There should be one grate per floor so you will need to account for that many replacments. The other very basic thing that you can clean is your drains by using a chemical like Draino and while you are cleaning your drains make sure to clean your faucets and showerheads because the mineral deposits can build up and cause your water flow to be very poor. The last thing you want to do around your house is check all of your safety equipment which includes things like smoke detectors and fire extinguishers, etc.

When To Call A Professional

If you don't feel confident doing a repair on your house then your best course of action is to call a professional so you don't make the situation worse.

  • Leaks: If your roof is ever leaking DO NOT try and fix this yourself unless you are a professional because you may end up making the problem worse. What you should do is turn the water off in your house and then call a plumber.
  • Roof Damage: Do not go onto your roof if you have never worked on a roof before because it is dangerous and the money you think you are saving will get spent and then some when you fall off the building.
  • Electrical Issues: If it is something simple like changing a regular wall plug then maybe you can tackle that issue yourself, but when it comes to very strong currents it's best to leave that to the pros because you could die.
